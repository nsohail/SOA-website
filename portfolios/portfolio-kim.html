About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

I am from Seoul, Korea. I love minimal art and so, I try to make my designs minimal when I work. Other than graphic design, I also love photography and music. Taking pictures has always been something that I have also enjoyed doing as a hobby. In the future, I hope to work as a graphic designer in the music industry in Korea to fulfill my passion for music and art.

I moved to America when I was young that I don’t have many memories from Korea. My brief life in Korea all felt like a dream; it was like living a new life in America. I only saw Korea through the screen—in Korean TV shows—and that was the only “access” to Korea that I had. I went to visit Korea a couple of times, but I never truly got to enjoy the trip because I wasn’t old enough to go around places by myself and also because I didn’t have much knowledge about Korea to get around places. By writing this book, Seoultastic, I want readers, including myself, to learn more about Korea so that this guidebook comes in handy when traveling there.

Seoultastic is a travel guidebook for South Korea that gives you all the information you need to know to travel around this country. This guidebook will educate you about Korea’s social etiquette, different types of foods and entertainment, how the transportation system works, and much more. Each chapter includes tips and advice to help you become a knowledgeable foreigner. By the end of this book, you will not only be well informed about South Korea, but you will also experience an enjoyable trip.
