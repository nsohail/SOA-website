About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

Matter is a visual exploration into the fundamentals of Physics. Physics is the study of matter and energy (electricity, force, light and movement) and how they interact with one another. Physics allows for research in other scientific studies, such as chemistry and biology, with the purpose to examine and measure behavior within the universe. The actions of physics are prevalent in our everyday lives, but regularly go unnoticed and unknowingly labeled as phenomena. This book will break down and explain complex Physics theories and processes through easy-to-understand explanations and simple forms.

I believe that every visual form should serve a specific function. Designers have the power to comprehend visually and spread information to educate and inform. I hope this book is a catalyst to trigger children’s interest in learning about Science and Physics.
