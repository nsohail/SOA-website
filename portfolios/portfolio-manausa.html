About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Drawing)

Minor Conflict Analysis and Resolution

Degree BA Art and Visual Technology

To me, art is all about leaving a deep impression on the hearts and minds of all who view it; to have the viewer walk away with no answers, but many questions. Of all the things in my life I hold dear, my faith is the one thing that requires constant attention and seems to always be in a state of fluid change. Faith is not an object one can possess, but an ongoing journey that we walk throughout our lives. My work reflects that journey, both my own and that of others.

My thought process for a creating art is all over the place. I will start a drawing, then another, then three others, and come back to the first, and then sometimes never finish it at all. This process wasn’t formed out of habit nor is it even intentional, but I create art based around what is happening in my life, what is happening around me, what is important to me, and these things constantly change.

I want to reflect the trials, tribulations and triumphs that come with putting something first in your life, whether that be your faith, yourself, your job, an addiction. Religion is what we make it to be and not necessarily devotion to a god.
