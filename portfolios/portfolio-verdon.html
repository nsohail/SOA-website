About Me

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

The Temple of Transformance is an installation that opens on April 21, 2015. It is located on George Mason University’s Fairfax campus. At the heart of this art work, it is a peace building structure. This Temple will bring people together and create a space for reflection and healing. Participants are invited to share their stories of remembrance, loss, fear, and forgiveness on its walls. On the night of May 8, 2015 at 8:30pm while it is floating on Mason Pond, we will transform the Temple from tangible object into collective memory with fire. In this final action, when solid wood becomes ash, individual and communal transformation is often possible. The sound of crackling fire will dance across the water while we stand motionless in silent reflection. It becomes a vessel to relinquish our feelings of fear and loss so we may move forward and heal together.
