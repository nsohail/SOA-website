About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

I’m a designer who is always looking to explore new places and gain inspiration for what I design. I love to travel, and among my many hobbies, I feel that traveling is a way of exploring new cultures, places, and more importantly art. Traveling gives me inspiration and fuels my creative spirit.

I have always had a passion for art and this eventually led me to study graphic design. My passion for design is always growing and one day I hope to work for an advertising or design agency.

I wrote a book about Bolivia’s largest annual carnival. It features insight on customary dances and their history, culture, and uniforms from around the country that are worn and presented for this event. This annual carnival or festival lasts for up to ten days and it comes about every February or March. It is called El Carnaval de Oruro and it takes place in the city of Oruro, Bolivia. This book features a lot of photography and it is meant for travelers, tourists, and people interested in culture to take with them on a trip. I would like people who read my book to learn about the history and dances of Bolivia, and for it to spring some interest on those who do not know much about it.
