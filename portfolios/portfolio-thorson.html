Krystin Thorson

About Me

Majors Art and Visual Technology (Sculpture) + English (Culture Studies)

Degrees BA Art and Visual Technology + English

I am a double Bachelor of Arts major in English and Art & Visual Technology. My concentrations are Cultural Studies and Sculpture, respectively. In my work, I am exploring the topic of identity within the home. I am exploring what it means to grow up in a home environment and how much our families affect our lives. What does it mean, for example, when the home base is taken away? How much of that home base will always stay with you, and how much falls away? I am also exploring the concept of loss within the family unit. Not loss in the form of death, but loss in the form of distance. I have grown up in a very beautiful, closely-knit, and strong family, but it is beginning to be disbanded by distance. I am exploring sadness and change that comes with it.
