From a young age I knew I wanted to follow in my uncle’s footsteps and pursue a career in design. I have been working since I was ten years old and my work ethic has not quit me yet. I have recently held an internship doing SEO, social media, and small business marketing, as well as an ongoing internship in print production. I also hold an officer position for the AIGA student group at George Mason as the Design Firm Manager.
