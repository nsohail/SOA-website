About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

I am a Korean American college student working towards becoming a graphic designer. My educational background includes a BFA in interior design from Pratt Institute, New York, a BFA in interior design from Virginia Commonwealth University and a BFA in graphic design from George Mason University. For the creative research I focus on the socio-cultural and socio-environmental contexts. My projects combine photography, fine arts, graphic design and sculpture. I am interested in combining graphic design with urban landscape and dynamic environments. I have developed designs for packaging, logo and stationery including book cover designs. As a student of various art degree programs, I became interested in communication art because unlike the study of architecture or interior design, which involves lot of limitations from code and regulatory requirements, graphic design seemed to lean more towards the creative side. I loved the idea of being able to communicate my thoughts or concepts through 2D design. You can influence people with the use of words, colors, shapes, lines and pictures. I hope to influence society and become an artist and designer who can inspire others. Currently I teach drawing and painting to young high school students and adults. After graduating I hope to become a freelance graphic designer and also an artist. My book is for the inexperienced puppy owners who are new to puppy training and also for the dog owners who want to train their dogs to behave. As a new puppy owner myself, I came to realize that raising a puppy is not as easy as you think. It takes a lot of effort and care, especially when they are newborn puppies. No matter how much you try, many pet owners feel overwhelmed by the biting, pooping, and barking problems. This is why the Good Puppy training guide book gives educational information and fun facts about our new friends. From the type of breeds, to vet care; it will educate the owners to raise smart and healthy puppies. This book combines my personal experiences from raising my two puppies Choco and Brownie. In order to reflect the cuteness of our loving dogs, I used warm colors for the layout of the pages. The images and text of the layout of the book mimic the connection of humans to nature.
