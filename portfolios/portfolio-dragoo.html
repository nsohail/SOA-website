Lauren Dragoo

About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Minor Art History

Degree BA Art and Visual Technology

I am as unique as they come and have had the opportunity to experience an extraordinary life for someone my age. I was born into a military family in Athens, Greece and spent my beginnings completely submerged in European culture. There, I developed my creativity and had the gift of drawing since the moment I could pick up a pencil. In my elementary years, I moved to my parents' native Texas until I reached high school when I found myself in the DC Metro area. After graduating high school, I tried out college and realized that I was not ready to settle down and focus just yet. In 2006, I dropped everything, got married and took off to the Mojave Desert where I bounced around California and the West Coast for a few years until duty and honor called my husband back to the DC area. I felt the itch to get back to school and decided to pursue graphic design at George Mason University. Nowadays, I am taking life by the horns and juggling motherhood with full-time school and waiting for the wind to blow me in a brand new direction.

During my formative years, I had the chance to discover so many different cultures around the world. These days, I am particularly interested in the brewery culture that is sweeping across the nation. Our neighboring state, North Carolina, prides itself as being the state that has the most breweries and brewpubs out of the entire south. With food and travel being one of my favorite pastimes, I wanted to experience these North Carolina breweries, document my journey and share it with the world.
