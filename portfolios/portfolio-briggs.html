About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BA Art and Visual Technology

I was born and raised in Michigan until age 18, when I headed to North Carolina with my boyfriend, who is now my husband, where we raised two healthy children—a son and a daughter.

I got my GED and ran my own construction company building custom homes around the Lake Norman area for about 10 years, until 9-11 happened, then we closed our business. I have an Associates of Science in Computer Programming and now a Bachelors of Arts in Graphic Design.

My style of design tends to be illusion—this works great in games and in modifying existing files for what I desire when creating game themes. I enjoy creating game design concepts in my free time. I have done scripts in the past but have gotten away from games at the moment and wish to one day go back since I enjoy the challenges of working with the code and the imagery which can be quite hair pulling but when it all comes together and works, it is quite rewarding.

My intention with this book—Family Favorites in 2 hours or less—is to be able to share with family those recipes that have been altered or passed down within the family. Also to show how one can work around food allergies and not miss out on foods that are enjoyable.
