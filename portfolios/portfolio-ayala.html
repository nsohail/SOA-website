About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (New Media)

Minor Computer Game Design

Degree BA Art and Visual Technology

For my senior project, I wanted to show the process of creating an animation. A lot of people focus on the finished product, but forget about the work that goes behind the scenes and how much work goes into its preparation. The preparation includes a story, character designs, storyboards, etc. I wanted to take this opportunity to familiarize myself with the beginning stages of animation. My work features an unusual protagonist: a young woman of color with arm and leg prosthetics. The story focuses on her saving her father while her relationship with him was dwindling. My intention is to learn the process, to improve, have my characters and their situations be relatable, and show the audience that there is so much more to animation than just the final product.
