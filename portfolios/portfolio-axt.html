About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (New Media)

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

From Out My Eyes seeks to explore the medium of sequential art by addinganimated elements and non-linear methods of storytelling. I want to approach a traditional comic format and expand, poke, and prod all of the little nuances that contribute to how we consume sequential art. By using animations, abstract loops and panels, as well as the formatting of a website, I can deliver a story in new ways rather than simply telling it. Inspired by the idea of the infinite canvas, where digital mediums allow for art that can be of any size, I want to add elements that traditionally wouldn’t be there. This piece culminates a combination of my own exploration and desire to combine the world of comics and website designs, as well as my own experiences with depression and anxiety. I wanted to create a cognitive battleground with my piece, one that both disrupts the reader as they read it, while also trying to have a conversation with my lesser angels. This piece is unique in that it is a part of a small genre of motion comics, or comics that seek to use motion as a way to influence the ebb and flow of a story. I am creating this comic in an effort to understand the effects of different animations on the cadence of a story. While a work of fiction, this comic is an expression of my own emotional battles delivered in a style that I have developed in spite of them
