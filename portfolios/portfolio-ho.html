Vynha Ho

About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

As a graphic designer, I communicate ideas and emotions through my work. Born and raised in Vietnam, Iwas able to adapt the culture diversity into my work. At the age of 7, I spent most of my free time reading and sketching small comic stories and characters. As I grew older I developed more interest in storytelling and graphic art.

I work telling stories and communicating abstract concepts in my work whether it is websites, ads, or logos. When I work on a project my style is generally bold yet simple. My goal is to hold people’s attention through my color choices, typography or formation of the work. I often find myself seeking inspiration from my surroundings. There’s always something intriguing that catches my eye, whether it is how a particular food is packaged or how a publication piece is designed.

Just like other designers, I dream of becoming successful and still loving what I want to do. I want to continue adapting the diverse cultures of art and graphics. As I work my way into the graphic design world, I want to experience new challenges. I enjoy solving problems and coming up with creative solutions. I constantly look for things to fix and improve.

Makeup is my way of creating art. Similar to my design, my makeup work tells stories. Every face has astory, and as a freelance makeup artist, my job is to bring that story into life. Using colors, textures and techniques, I empower each of the individual faces. Through each brush stroke, I help women discover their outer beauty as well as inner beauty. I believe that when you look better, you will feel better. With positive attitudes, women will have the ability to change their lives positively. I want to share my knowledge about makeup and skincare with readers. It has been a part of me for the last 8 years, and with everything I’ve learned and experienced, I have been able to grow as an artist. This book—Polaroids of Beauty—is where art and design meet.
