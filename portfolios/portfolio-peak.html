About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BFA Art and Visual Technology

I enjoy being in nature and exploring new places, which is why I love to hike, travel and experience different cultures. My desire for adventure shines through in my work as a graphic designer. Which led me to the topic of my book: Best Hiking Trails at Great Falls.Great Falls is an area that I have been going to since I was a kid. When I was young I would go with my parents and see the views of the waterfalls. My young experiences there became the stepping-stones for my love of hiking. The Great Falls area runs right through Maryland and Virginia, separated by the Potomac River. Both the Great Falls National Park (VA) and the C&O Canal National Park (MD) offer all sorts of hiking trails, from walking to great views, to climbing up cliffs. I have always considered the Great Falls hiking trails to be a sacred gem encompassed by the fast-paced lifestyle of suburban DC. My book highlights the most adventurous trails at Great Falls. Not only will the trail guides prepare you for a hike, but they will also get you excited to embark on your adventure!
