About Me

Major Art and Visual Technology (Graphic Design)

Degree BA Art and Visual Technology

I am a designer and illustrator. I came from China—the experience of studying overseas leads me to consider about different cultural issues. As a result, I am trying to balance the traditional Chinese culture and the contemporary American Art in my work. Meanwhile, I hope my work can express my feelings, dreams, and life experiences. I want to inspire others to discover their own passion and to put it in their own artistic work as well. If you want to know who am I, just let my work speak for me.

The main goal of my travel book is to design for my own usage. Therefore, this book is more than a travel guide—I want to show the audiences my stories, experiences, and my work of art. Get to know the U.S.—from east to west (the title of my book) is more like a personal diary that records everything about me in the past three years such as the places I have been to, the people I have met, and the things I have done. As a result, I show my own images in this book and talk about my stories rather than just giving information about the different cities I went to.

I wanted to make this book different from other projects I have done before, so I decided to play with geometric shapes. For example, to make a folded page for a map or to have an interleaf that gives space to take notes. The book is not only well-designed with elegant illustrations, but also a functional guidebook.
