AVT SPRING 2015 Exhibition

About The Show

Please join the George Mason University School of Visual Arts’ graduating class in the Spring 2015’s Art & Design Senior Show. The exhibition is a collective of painting, drawing, new media, graphic design, sculpture, photography, printmaking and interdisciplinary arts.
The show opens Monday, May 4th, with closing reception on Friday, May 8th from 6-9 PM
